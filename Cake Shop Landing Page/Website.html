ABOUT US
Cakes ‘n’ Bakes was established in 1992 with a single shop in Ambawadi area of Ahmedabad city. Since then we have grown and now we have chain of Shops & Franchisees in Ahmedabad. Many agencies are associated with us which collect cakes from us and deliver in Ahmedabad and nearby places. Some of the agencies that are involved are ahmedabad.com, ahmedabadcity.com, Gujarat Gifts, G.M. Gifts, RAMI Bros., and many more. We are specialized for eggless vegetarian cake. Our customers get more than 100 photographs and designs of different cakes to choose from for occasions like Birthday, Engagement, Ring Ceremony, Wedding, Reception, Get together, Valentine, Diwali, etc. It’s like ‘You Name it, We have it’ when it comes to the kind of cakes we make.
