Queen's Necklace : Marine Drive

Marine Drive is a 3 kilometre-long Promenade along the Netaji Subhash Chandra Bose Road in Mumbai,India.
The road and promenade were constructed by Pallonji Mistry. It is a 'C'-shaped six-lane concrete road along
the coast of a natural bay. At the northern end of Marine Drive is Girgaon Chowpatty and the adjacent road
along links Nariman Point at southern tip to Babulnath and Malabar Hill at northern tip.
Marine Drive is also known as the Queen's Necklace because, when viewed at night from an elevated
point anywhere along the drive, the street lights resemble a string of pearls in a necklace.
Marine Drive is situated on reclaimed land facing west-south-west.
