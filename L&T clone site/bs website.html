Businesses

Construction

L&T Construction is among the world’s top contractors.
The business encompasses multiple business -
Buildings & Factories, Transport Infrastructure,
Heavy Civil Infrastructure, Smart World & Communication,
Water & Renewable Energy and Power Transmission & Distribution.

Construction & Mining Machinery

L&T’s construction equipment business manufactures,
distributes and provides after-sales support for
construction and mining equipment.

Hydrocarbon

L&T offers integrated ‘design to build’ solutions to
offshore and onshore hydrocarbon projects worldwide.
It offers concept-to-commissioning services across
the hydrocarbon value chain.

Power

L&T provides integrated concept - to - commissioning
solutions for supercritical thermal power plants.
We also offer comprehensive services for gas-based
power plants.

ABOUT L&T GROUP


Headquartered in Mumbai, Larsen & Toubro Limited is one of the
largest and most respected companies in India's private sector.
With over 80 years of a strong, customer focused approach and
a continuous quest for world-class quality, L&T has unmatched
capabilities across Technology, Engineering, Construction,
and Manufacturing, and maintains a leadership in all its major
lines of business.
